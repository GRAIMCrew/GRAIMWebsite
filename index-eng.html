ABOUT

GRAIM Studio is the Electronic Music school of the Vicenza Conservatory,
part of the department of New Technologies and Musical Languages.
The bachelor course started in 2003, while the interactivity/multimedia masters course started in 2018.
The production studio and the variety of educational offerings allow students to best express their creative ideas in freedom.
